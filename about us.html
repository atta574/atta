About Nextag Bags

Where Elegance Meets Craftsmanship

Founded in 2010, LuxeBags began as a small atelier in Milan with a vision to create handbags and wallets that combine timeless elegance with exceptional quality. What started as a passion project has grown into a globally recognized brand, beloved by women who appreciate fine leather goods.

Each LuxeBags product is meticulously crafted by our team of skilled artisans who bring decades of experience to every stitch. We source only the finest materials from ethical suppliers around the world, ensuring that every piece meets our exacting standards.

Our designs blend classic silhouettes with contemporary details, creating accessories that are both fashionable and enduring. Whether you're looking for an everyday wallet or a statement handbag, LuxeBags offers pieces that will elevate your style for years to come.
